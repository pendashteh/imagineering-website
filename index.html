// about

Vibe Coding Night is a regular build session where humans and AI agents build things together. Bring an idea, a half-finished project, or just show up and plug into someone else's.

Three focused 25-minute sprints, demos at the end, good conversation in between. Collaboration over competition. Something real gets made every time.
